About

The future of learning is intelligent, connected and pervasive in everyday life. The nature of learning is fundamentally shaping with the scale of change in our world - the ICT revolution, world economic growth, and the emphasis on the skills to thrive in them. As part of the Innovative Computing Group, housed in Department of Computer Science at Durham University, the Learning Lab is committed to improving and positively impacting on the society by pushing the boundaries of sciences and technologies of learning which are creating opportunities for transforming learning in ways that were unimaginable in the past.
