The future of learning is intelligent, connected and pervasive in everyday life. Here in the Learning Lab, we are sparking new research that will deepen our understanding of learning and fundamentally change how humans and machines learn.

About

Housed in the Department of Computer Science, Durham University, the is committed to improving and positively impacting on the society by pushing the boundaries of sciences and technologies of learning which create opportunities for transforming learning in ways that were unimaginable in the past. We are particularly interested in developing learning theories and designing intelligent systems that facilitate collaboration and co-creation between humans and machines, with ethical, legal and societal values. We aim at implementing an approach to Human-centred Artificial Intelligence and shaping the AI revolution.
